Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you teach?

We teach students from Grade 5 to 12, covering a wide range of subjects tailored to different levels.

Fees vary depending on the course and duration. Please contact us for detailed information.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online classes to accommodate different learning preferences.

You can enroll by contacting us through the contact form or calling us directly. We will guide you through the enrollment process.

We offer a variety of courses across different subjects to help students succeed academically. Each course is designed to provide in-depth knowledge and practical skills.

Advanced Mathematics

This course covers advanced mathematical concepts including calculus, algebra, and geometry. Ideal for high school students preparing for college-level mathematics.

Duration: 12 weeks

Instructor: Raj Patel

Introduction to Physics

A foundational course in physics covering basic principles and laws of motion. Suitable for beginners and those looking to refresh their understanding of fundamental concepts.

Duration: 8 weeks

Instructor: Sheetal Sharma

English Literature

Explore classic and modern literature, focusing on analysis and interpretation. This course enhances reading comprehension and critical thinking skills.

Duration: 10 weeks

Instructor: Raj Patel
